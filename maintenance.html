Seasonal schedule
DFW seasonal maintenance calendar

Dallas-Fort Worth's extreme heat, storms, and clay soil create unique maintenance needs throughout the year.

Spring (March–May)
  • Schedule HVAC tune-up before June heat
  • Clean dryer duct — lint builds over winter
  • Check washing machine hoses for cracks
  • Clean refrigerator coils after heating season
  • Test smoke and CO detectors
Summer (June–Aug)
  • Change AC filters every 30 days — DFW dust
  • Keep 2 ft clearance around outdoor AC unit
  • Flush dishwasher drain monthly — high usage
  • Check refrigerator door seals weekly
  • Flush water heater to remove sediment
Fall (Sept–Nov)
  • Deep clean oven before holiday cooking season
  • Inspect and clean washing machine gasket
  • Test all GFCI outlets in kitchen and bathroom
  • Clean range hood filters
  • Check foundation for summer drought cracks
Winter (Dec–Feb)
  • Insulate pipes — DFW freezes are sudden
  • Check dryer vent for bird nests
  • Clean refrigerator coils — heating system adds dust
  • Test dishwasher water temperature (should be 120°F)
  • Check washing machine for cold-weather hose cracks
